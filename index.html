Overview

Structured support for complex financial operating models.

Financial Technology Advisory Services is a South African advisory firm operating across fintech, payments and financial infrastructure. The firm helps teams align commercial ambition with regulatory expectations, governance routines and practical delivery control.

The work is built for banks, fintechs, neobanks, payment intermediaries and embedded finance teams that need a clearer path from opportunity to launch readiness and run-state assurance.

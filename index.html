Compliance and risk readiness

Structured readiness assessments, governance preparation and specialist referral.

FTAS helps fintechs, payment providers, embedded finance businesses and other financial innovators understand and prepare for the compliance, risk and governance expectations associated with sponsor bank partnerships and market participation.

Through structured assessments, FTAS evaluates partner suitability, documentation readiness, commercials including pricing models, governance arrangements, operating model maturity and potential risk gaps that may affect onboarding or partnership approval.

We help clients strengthen their readiness by identifying required actions, improving supporting documentation, clarifying governance responsibilities and preparing for engagement with banks, regulators and partners.

Where detailed compliance, risk management, regulatory implementation or ongoing monitoring support is required, FTAS facilitates a structured digital handover to Elysian Compliance & Risk Management for specialist advisory and implementation services.
